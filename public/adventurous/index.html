Adventurous

Your Daily Adventure, Delivered.

Every day is an opportunity to discover something new. Adventurous is an iOS app that gives you a fresh adventure recommendation each day — personalized to your schedule, your preferences, and where you are.

It’s not a travel app. It’s not a social network. It’s something different: a small nudge to step outside your routine and see what’s around you.

What to Expect

  • A new adventure every day — no two days the same
  • Personalized to your life — respects whether it’s a work day or a day off
  • Local and nearby — adventures scaled to how far you can realistically go
  • Points of interest and weather — everything you need to decide if today’s adventure is the right one
