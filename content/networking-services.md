## Services

### 🔌 Structured Cabling
Clean, organized cabling is the foundation of a reliable network. We run and terminate **Cat5e, Cat6, and fiber optic** cable — new installations, rewiring, and cleanup of existing runs. We leave walls, ceilings, and equipment rooms looking professional.

### 📶 Wireless Networking
Professional-grade **Ubiquiti** Wi-Fi solutions for businesses that need solid coverage, not consumer-grade access points that drop connections during a video call. We design, install, and configure wireless networks to cover your entire space — including dead zones.

### ⚙️ Network Installation & Configuration
From switches and routers to firewalls and patch panels — we install and configure complete network infrastructure. New setups or upgrades to existing equipment, done right the first time.

### 🔍 Network Troubleshooting
Slow network? Intermittent drops? Devices that won't connect? We diagnose and fix network problems with real tools, not guesswork.

### 🛡️ Network Security
Basic network security goes a long way. We configure firewalls, set up **VLAN segmentation** to isolate sensitive systems (like point-of-sale hardware or security cameras) from general traffic, and review your network for common vulnerabilities.

### 🗄️ Server & NAS Setup
Need centralized file storage, a local backup system, or an on-site server? We set up and configure **Windows Server, Linux, and NAS devices** (Synology, QNAP) sized appropriately for small business workloads.

### 📋 Network Assessments & Planning
Not sure what you have or what you need? We'll assess your current setup, document it, and give you a clear picture of where you stand — and a practical plan for where to go next. No upselling, no unnecessary complexity.

### 🤝 Ongoing Support
We offer **monthly support agreements** for businesses that want a reliable point of contact when something goes wrong. Flat-rate or time-based, depending on your needs.
