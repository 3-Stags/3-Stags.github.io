We've been working on something new.

**Adventurous** is an iOS app built around a simple idea: every day should have at least one small adventure. Not a vacation. Not a road trip. Just a reason to get out of your usual routine and explore something nearby that you wouldn't have found on your own.

The app gives you a fresh adventure location every single day — different each time, calibrated to your schedule, and ready to go when you are. Check the weather, see what's around, and decide if today is the day you go.
